Here's a Bit About Us.

Why do we do what we do?

A simple question, but it really defines everything. It's the first question we ask our clients and a question we have to constantly ask ourselves. And just like our clients, we have a passion for our craft - it's why we get up in the morning.

Beasley Creative folk are a unique breed. Sure, we have to pay the bills, but at the end of the day we really love what we do. Our clients and users are at the forefront of our priorities and when we really nail a project, it's all worth it.

We do what we do because we love to help our clients share their vision with the world (while throwing in a bit of fun on the way).

Current Opportunities

The BeaCrea team is tightly knit and highly collaborative. We ride together, we die together.

Here's who we're currently looking for.

Creative Front End Developer/Designer

"I actively create sites that push the envelope and look great. I love to experiment!"

Looking for a sharp front-end developer that can take what's thrown their way and make it their own. We're looking for Wordpress experience, mostly template creation, as well as the HTML5/CSS3/Javascript basics. Looking for some decent javascript experience as well in order to have some cool interactions built into our projects. Needs to have an eye for design, even if they don't design much themselves and be able to think on the fly.

This is a 1099 contractor position with a variable workload.
Need to see past work/side projects and demostrate strong problem solving.

Requirements

  • Semantic HTML5
  • CSS3 Experience
  • Javascript and/or Jquery
  • Responsive Design(Must!)
  • Familiarity with Bootstrap/HTML5BP
  • An Interesting and Fun Portfolio

Vector Illustrator

"I can tell a story with fun characters and environments that have personality."

Looking for a designer with experience in vector illustration. Needs to be fluent/expert in inDesign/Photoshop/Illustrator. All in all, we're looking for someone that can tell a story and convey information whether its a bunch of characters or an infographic.

This is a 1099 contractor position with a variable workload.
Must have a strong portfolio and show storytelling in design.

Requirements

  • inDesign/Photoshop/Illustrator Fluency
  • Prepress Preferred
  • Web Design Experience a Plus
  • Mobile Design a Plus
  • Drawing/Illustration Skills
  • Able to Adapt to Different Styles
  • An Interesting and Fun Portfolio

Internships

"I'm looking to get my feet wet and learn, whether its account management, design, or grabbing breakfast."

We're always looking for fresh talent. If you have a willing attitude, are ambitious, don't mind picking up a book or running through screencasts, and above all have great communication and social interaction, then let us know. We're more than willing to turn internships into permanent positions with time and potential.

Must have a great attitude and have some experience in programming, design, film, or illustration.
This is typically an unpaid position.

Requirements

  • Great Personality
  • Ambitious and Driven
  • Design/Development Experience a Plus
  • Eager to Learn
